- **Alignment with SOC Workflows**: The revised bullets now clearly reflect the entry-level SOC Analyst role, focusing on tasks like monitoring and triaging security alerts, conducting investigations, gathering evidence, and creating detailed documentation.

- **Clarity and Precision**: The revised bullets are more concise and precise, making it easier for recruiters to quickly identify relevant skills and experiences.
