### Explanation of Changes

**Detection**: This term is used to align with the job description’s focus on identifying security events and anomalies, which is crucial in SOC roles.

**Triage**: Highlighting triage skills ensures that candidates understand how to prioritize and classify alerts effectively, a key aspect of entry-level SOC responsibilities.

**Investigation**: Emphasizing investigation helps demonstrate the candidate's ability to delve deeper into incidents, analyze data, and uncover root causes—skills essential for comprehensive incident response.

**Documentation**: Stressing documentation is important as it reflects the candidate’s ability to communicate findings clearly, which is vital in both security analysis and user support roles.

**Escalation**: Including escalation demonstrates awareness of when to seek further assistance, aligning with the need to follow procedures and collaborate effectively within an organization.

By focusing on these specific skills and experiences, the resume becomes more aligned with the target job description and showcases the candidate's readiness for entry-level SOC analyst roles.
