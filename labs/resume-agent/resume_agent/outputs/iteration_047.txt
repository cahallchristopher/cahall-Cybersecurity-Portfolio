### Explanation of Changes

- **Alignment with SOC Workflows:** The revised bullets now focus on the specific tasks and workflows mentioned in the target job description, such as monitoring, triage, investigation, and documentation.
- **Use of Evidence-Based Claims:** Each bullet includes concrete examples or evidence derived from lab work, ensuring they can be defended if asked for details.
- **Emphasis on SOCTools and Techniques:** Keywords have been included to highlight relevant skills and experience that align with the job requirements.
- **Removed Overly Technical Terms:** Buzzwords like "payload reverse engineering" have been omitted as per the constraints. Instead, we focused on observable behaviors and outcomes.
- **Clear and Concise Language:** The language is now more aligned with what an entry-level SOC analyst would need to demonstrate in their role, focusing on practical skills and real-world applications.
