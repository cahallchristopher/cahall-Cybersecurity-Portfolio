3. **SOC Workflows:**

4. **Technical Focus:**

5. **Evidence-Driven Language:**

6. **Clear Communication:**

By making these changes, the resume now more accurately reflects the skills and experiences relevant to an entry-level SOC Analyst position while adhering strictly to the provided constraints.
