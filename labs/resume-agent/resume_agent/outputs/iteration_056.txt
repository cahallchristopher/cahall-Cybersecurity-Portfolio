### Explanation of Changes

**Detection and Triage:** This bullet focuses on the ability to monitor security alerts, perform initial triage based on severity indicators, which directly aligns with the job description's responsibility of monitoring and investigating alerts.

**Investigation and Analysis:** By using specific tools like Wireshark and Splunk for analysis, this bullet showcases practical experience in analyzing network traffic patterns and endpoint behavior, demonstrating a strong understanding of investigative techniques required in SOC roles.

**Documentation and Reporting:** The emphasis on creating detailed reports that document findings ensures that candidates are prepared to communicate technical information effectively, which is crucial for roles requiring clear documentation practices.

**Escalation and Collaboration:** Highlighting the ability to escalate high-severity incidents appropriately demonstrates an understanding of proper incident response procedures and teamwork, key elements in SOC roles.

By focusing on these specific tasks and outcomes, the revised resume bullets align more closely with the job description, emphasizing practical experience in detection, triage, investigation, documentation, and escalation.
