---------------------------------------------------------------
LOGGING & VISIBILITY
---------------------------------------------------------------
• All DNS requests pass through Ubuntu Gateway
• Vendor activity is logged and attributable
• No direct internet access from internal systems
• Gateway logs support audits and SOC review

---------------------------------------------------------------
SOC QUESTIONS THIS DIAGRAM ANSWERS
---------------------------------------------------------------
• Where does vendor traffic enter the network?
• What activity is logged?
• What system enforces policy?
• Where would alerts be generated?
• Could this pass a compliance audit?

---------------------------------------------------------------
DESIGN PRINCIPLE
---------------------------------------------------------------
If traffic does not pass through the gateway,
it is considered unmonitored and non-compliant.
